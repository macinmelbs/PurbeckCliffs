Apartment with Indoor Pool, Sauna & Snooker Room in Swanage

Purbeck Cliffs is a spacious ground-floor apartment with its own private indoor heated pool, sauna and snooker room. Nestled beside Durlston Country Park and the Jurassic Coast, it's set within 1.5 acres of landscaped gardens in a quiet cul-de-sac — just 1 mile from Swanage town centre and the award-winning blue flag beach.

Perfect for families, couples or friends, the apartment sleeps up to 6 and features:

  • bed Two bedrooms – a twin room, and a family room with double bed + bunk beds
  • pool 40’ indoor heated pool with slide, sun beds and direct access to patio
  • hot_tub Sauna and separate shower/relaxation room
  • sports_esports Full-size snooker table
  • tv Sun lounge with TV, DVD player and board games
  • kitchen Fully equipped kitchen and indoor/outdoor dining areas
  • park Landscaped private gardens & barbecue patio area

You're within walking distance of events like the Jazz Festival, Folk Festival, Steam & Classic Vehicle Show, and Purbeck International Chamber Music Festival.
